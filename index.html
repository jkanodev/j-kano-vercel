About J-Kano

Calm design. Serious execution.

Your site should answer questions fast, reduce friction, and make it obvious you’re a business worth hiring—not a side project buried in effects.

Who we are

J-Kano is a small, senior-led web studio—not a volume shop. We work with owners and operators who are done with template noise and want a site that matches the quality of what they sell in the real world.

That means restraint where it matters, typography and spacing you can trust, and structure that supports revenue—not decoration for its own sake.

What we deliver

Marketing sites, landing systems, service-area pages, menus, product storytelling, and—when you’re ready—custom admin surfaces so your team can publish without bottlenecks.

We’re selective about fit. If you want the lowest-cost possible page, we’re probably not it. If you want something you’d put next to a national brand and feel proud, that’s the standard we hold.
